Scene 1

Redwood Lake
December 21, 2015 – 5:00 PM
Grimwood, Jefferson

A father and his son are walking near Redwood Lake, talking casually.

The son notices someone sitting against the wall of a distant building, head resting on the wall, as if sleeping.

The son tells his father he will go wake the man up.

The father notices two or three people approaching from the opposite direction.

Something feels strange, so the father calls his son back before he can go any further.

The father then sees one of those men lifting the sleeping person onto his shoulder and carrying him away.

The father panics and orders his son to go straight home.

Both of them leave the area quickly.

Scene 2

Mavean Street, Grimwood, Jefferson
5:20 PM

An English tuition class is in session.

The teacher is teaching while joking and interacting with the students.

A boy’s phone rings.

He asks the teacher if he can take the call outside. She agrees.

Outside the classroom, he answers the call.

A man on the other side says:
“Come near Redwood Lake immediately. They’re coming today. All three of them.”

The boy’s face turns serious. He replies, “Okay.”

He returns to the class and tells the teacher he has an urgent issue at home and must leave.

The teacher allows him to go and tells him to be careful.

The boy goes home, packs a bag, and leaves.

The whole class watches him.

A boy sitting in the last row watches him very carefully.

The boy leaves quietly. The class resumes.

Scene 3

Grimwood Police Station
5:30 PM

Inside the station, officers are busy with their work.

In one room, a senior officer named Ray is joking and chatting with junior officers.

An officer enters and reports a call from the Redwood area about suspicious activity.

Ray says sarcastically:
“I came to Grimwood for just one day, for five hours—and even then, work?”

He sighs and agrees to check it out.

Ray leaves with a few officers toward Redwood.

Scene 4

Near Redwood Lake
5:50 PM

The boy who left the tuition meets a man near the lake.

The man says:
“They’ll arrive soon. Climb that tree. You’ll get a clear target from above. Three shots. Kill all three.”

The man hands him a sniper rifle and reminds him to do exactly what he practiced in training.

The boy climbs the tree, takes position, and aims.

The man says he will go ahead to confirm their arrival and will message him.

He leaves.

Scene 5

Mavean Street, Grimwood, Jefferson
6:20 PM

The tuition class ends. Students pack their bags.

The teacher holds up a book and asks whose it is.

A student says it belongs to Reyji, who left earlier.

The teacher asks someone to deliver it to his house.

The boy who had been watching Reyji—Ardh—calls someone and says his tuition is over.

Another student suggests Ardh should deliver the book since Reyji lives nearby.

Ardh refuses.

The teacher firmly tells him to take it.

Annoyed, Ardh takes the book and exits.

Outside, Rick is waiting with his bicycle.

Ardh asks how he arrived so fast.

Rick says he has been waiting for an hour.

Ardh says there’s no hanging out today; he needs to return a book.

Rick insists on coming along.

They ride toward Reyji’s house.

Scene 6

6:40 PM

Officer Ray and his team are driving toward the Redwood area.

Ray asks who made the report.

An officer says it was a man named Kimson.

Ray asks what he saw.

The officer replies:
“He said some men were carrying someone away.”

Ray decides to meet Kimson first.

He sends the rest of the team to investigate Redwood Lake.

Scene 7

6:50 PM

Ardh and Rick are cycling toward Reyji’s house.

They notice a police van heading toward Redwood.

Rick wonders why police are there at this hour.

Ardh suggests ignoring it and delivering the book first.

Rick hesitates and suggests checking it out.

Ardh agrees but warns they must return soon.

They turn toward Redwood Lake.

Scene 8

7:00 PM

Reyji is sitting on a tree, waiting.

A police van passes below.

Three officers get out; the van drives away.

The officers begin searching the area.

Reyji watches silently from above.

Scene 9

7:10 PM

Ray reaches Kimson’s house.

Kimson explains what he saw around 5 PM.

Ray asks more questions and gives Kimson his card.

Kimson offers Ray a glass of water.

Scene 10

7:20 PM

Ardh and Rick arrive near Redwood Lake.

They discuss why the police might be there.

Ardh mentions rumors about the “Yellow Ghost.”

Rick gets scared and suggests leaving.

Ardh laughs it off and insists on checking.

Suddenly, Ardh notices someone watching them from a tree.

Rick keeps talking, but Ardh stares upward.

Ardh tells Rick to keep moving.

Rick looks up, panics, loses balance, and they both fall.

A figure climbs down from the tree and walks toward them.

Rick starts crying, calling it the Yellow Ghost.

The figure asks:
“You’re Ardh, right?”

Ardh replies in shock:
“Reyji?”

Rick blurts out:
“Ardh, you never told me Reyji was a ghost!”

Scene 11

7:30 PM

Rick, Ardh, and Reyji talk near the lake.

Ardh asks why Reyji was in the tree.

Reyji avoids the question and asks why they are there.

Ardh explains about the forgotten book.

Reyji questions why they took such a long route.

Rick senses something off.

Reyji claims he was searching for something.

Rick excuses himself to use the bathroom.

Moments later, a scream is heard.

Ardh and Reyji run to the bushes.

Rick’s slippers are on the ground.

Drag marks lead toward the abandoned building.

Ardh panics—Rick has been taken.

Reyji receives a message:
“All three have arrived. Take position.”

Reyji tells Ardh he has to leave.

Ardh storms off toward the building to find Rick.

Reyji climbs back into position, aiming at the building—but he’s shaken.

A car arrives. Three bodyguards and three gangsters enter the building.

Ray cannot contact his team.

Ray’s officers are knocked unconscious.

Ardh stands at the building’s back entrance.

Reyji is torn between two choices:
Save his friend—or follow his mission.
